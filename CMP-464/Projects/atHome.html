The Am staff at Home

Why should you get the Am Staff?

The am staff is a very energetic and affectionate breed of dog. Like all dogs, it is important to socialize and train them.
The am staff also has a very protective side. Training and socialization is also important for them to live well with children
and other domestic animals.

How important is exercise?

According to the American Kennel Club, as these dogs require a lot of exercise, leaving the dog in the yard all day is not the right approach:
yes, they can run, but it is also important to play with them for 1-2 hours. This develops their physical and psychological health.

How is the Shedding?

Jokes aside: because of their short coat, am staff dogs do not shed much hair.
In additon, grooming is also a lot easier due to their short coating.

Health Issues

According to the American Kennel Club, am staff can have hereditary complications such as:

  • Canine hip dysplasia
  • Hypothyroidism (a condition that affects thyroid hormones)
  • Heart disease and skin and coat allergies

What can we do?

The Club recommends the following health examinations:

  • Hip evaluation
  • Ophthalmologic evaluation
  • NCL DNA test
  • Cardiac Screening
  • Thyroid Evaluation
